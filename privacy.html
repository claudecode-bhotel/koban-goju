Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

When you book the Dashi & Sushi Experience, we collect personal information including your full name, email address, phone number, and booking details such as your preferred date and number of guests. Payment information is securely processed through Stripe and is not stored directly on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to process your booking, send confirmation emails, and provide customer support. We may also use your contact information to notify you of any changes to your reservation or to send you relevant updates about our services. We do not share your information with third parties except as necessary to fulfill your booking.

Third-Party Services

We use Stripe for secure payment processing and Google Calendar for scheduling your experience. These third-party service providers have their own privacy policies and are committed to protecting your data. We encourage you to review their privacy policies to understand how they handle your information.

Data Retention

We retain booking data for legal and tax compliance purposes for a period of seven years after your experience. If you wish to have your personal information deleted, you may request deletion at any time, and we will process your request in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at b hotel, located in Koban-dori Goju, Kyoto. We are committed to answering your questions and addressing any privacy concerns.
