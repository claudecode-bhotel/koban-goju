Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

When you make a reservation for the Dashi & Sushi Experience, we collect the following personal information: full name, email address, phone number, accommodation reservation number (Guesty booking ID), room details, number of guests, and allergy / dietary restriction information. This information is collected solely for the purpose of managing your reservation and ensuring a safe, personalized experience.

Payment Information

All payment processing is handled securely by Stripe, Inc. We do not store your credit or debit card information on our servers. When you proceed to payment, your card details are entered directly on Stripe's secure platform. Stripe registers your card at the time of booking, and the charge is automatically processed at 11:00 AM three days before your experience date. b hotel never has access to your full card number.

How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used exclusively to: confirm and manage your reservation; process payment via Stripe; send booking confirmation and reminder emails; accommodate allergy or dietary needs; and contact you in the event of changes to your reservation or cancellation. We do not use your information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent, and we do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except as required to fulfill your booking (Stripe for payments, Google Calendar for scheduling).

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services: Stripe for secure payment processing; Google Calendar for internal scheduling of your experience. Each of these providers operates under its own privacy policy and security standards. We encourage you to review Stripe's Privacy Policy (stripe.com/privacy) and Google's Privacy Policy (policies.google.com/privacy) to understand how your data is handled by these services.

Data Retention

We retain your reservation and personal data for a period of seven (7) years following your experience, in accordance with Japanese tax and accounting regulations. After this period, your data will be securely deleted. If you wish to request early deletion of your personal information, please contact us at the address below. We will process your request in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

Contact

For any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, or to request deletion of your personal data, please contact us at: support@breakfast-h.com. We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 5 business days.
